Core Capabilities

Everything Your Hotel
Needs, In One Place

From the moment a guest books to the moment they check out — and every step in between — SS PMS Cloud has it covered in a single, unified platform.

Front Desk & Reservations

Handle walk-ins, bookings, check-ins and check-outs from one clean screen. Smart booking lets you type a request in plain English and the system creates the reservation instantly.

Housekeeping

Real-time room status that front desk and housekeeping always agree on. Board and list views, one-tap updates, and strict business rules that prevent conflicting statuses.

Groups & Rooming

End-to-end group management — room blocks, pick-up tracking, rooming list reconciliation, pro-forma invoices and printable registration cards, all in one place.

Rate & Revenue

Contract rates auto-attach to company and agent bookings. Package allowances with next-day posting. Automated night audit posts room revenue and tax by code — no manual reconciliation.

Guest Documents

Branded booking confirmations, registration cards, A4 folio invoices and group pro-formas — polished, on-brand paperwork every time, without fiddly formatting.

Smart Reports

Type what you need in plain English — "arrivals next 7 days with balance," "rooming list for group Miramar" — and the system builds the report from live data. Download to CSV, save to History.

Smart Automation

Built-in Intelligence,
Not a Paid Add-on

Four smart assistants are built right into the platform — handling the repetitive, time-consuming tasks that have always eaten your team's hours.

01
Smart Reservations
Smart Booking & Walk-in

Type the request in plain English and the system creates the reservation — guest, dates, room type, rate and cashier note. Far fewer clicks than any legacy form.

  • "2 nights, deluxe king, breakfast, paying by the company" → done
  • Multi-room walk-ins with combined or separate confirmation
  • Auto-attaches company / agent contracted rate
02
Smart Front Office
Smart Room Assignment

Assign rooms by your rules in seconds — what used to be a manual jigsaw every morning. The system honours preferences, keeps parties together, and never double-books.

  • Accessibility needs → accessible rooms automatically
  • VIPs to the best rooms; honours high floor, quiet, view requests
  • Upgrades when the booked type is full
03
Smart Groups
Smart Group Rooming

Paste the organiser's final rooming list and the system reconciles it against the block — names and dates updated automatically. Preview every change before applying.

  • Matches updated guest names to the right rooms
  • Reconciles per-guest arrival / departure within the block
  • Locked after check-in — safe by design
04
Smart Reporting
Reports in Plain English

The standout feature: type what you want and the system builds the report from live data — then saves it to History to re-run anytime. No IT ticket. No waiting.

  • "Rooming list for group Miramar", "arrivals next 7 days with balance"
  • Reads live data — read-only and safe, never raw database access
  • Download any result to CSV instantly

Built to be Trusted

One Source of Truth,
With Guardrails

A hotel runs on accurate data. SS PMS Cloud is engineered so every screen, every role, and every transaction reads from the same single source — with rules enforced behind the scenes.

01
Consistent Everywhere

A room reads the same on the dashboard, front desk, housekeeping and reservations — no drift, no conflicting statuses, no "which screen do I trust?" moments.

02
Strict Business Rules

Can't take an occupied room out of order. Can't assign or check into an out-of-order room. These guardrails are enforced at the system level — not just a warning, a hard stop.

03
Graceful Failure

If anything unexpected happens, staff see a clean recover screen — never a blank or broken page. Your operations continue; your guests never feel a thing.

04
Automatic Backups

Data is snapshotted before changes. A one-command health check verifies every screen is green before you open the doors — or before a presentation to ownership.
