Core Capabilities

Built for How Hotel
Sales Teams Actually Work

Generic CRMs aren't built for hospitality. SuiteSupports CRM speaks your language — rate negotiations, group RFQs, market segments, RevPAR, and month-end reporting are all first-class features.

Sales Activity Tracking

Log every client interaction — calls, meetings, site inspections, proposals, and rate negotiations. Track deal status from Lead through to Won or Lost, with follow-up date reminders and deal value forecasting.

AI Group Quotation

Paste a group inquiry email and AI extracts all key details — agent, dates, rooms, rates, PAX, suggested pricing, rationale, and a ready-to-send reply draft. One click to populate the full quotation.

Production & Revenue

Full production reporting by company, travel agent, or all accounts. Current year vs. prior year comparison with revenue delta, production rankings, trend graphs, and CSV export.

Market Segmentation

Visualise revenue by main and sub-market segments with trend graphs and breakdown tables. Track budget allocation against actuals and identify which segments are over or underperforming.

History & Forecast

Daily occupancy, ADR, and RevPAR — past and forward. Room type distribution, revenue forecasts against plan, and demand anomaly alerts that flag opportunities before they're missed.

Campaign Management

Build and send targeted marketing campaigns across Email, SMS, and WhatsApp from your own guest database. Full HTML EDM builder, campaign archive, and performance tracking — no third-party tool required.

Standout Features

Two Things No Generic
CRM Can Match

AI-powered group quotation extraction and a built-in multi-channel campaign engine are hospitality-specific capabilities that set SuiteSupports CRM apart from every generic sales tool.

AI-Powered
Group Quotation Intelligence

Your sales team receives dozens of group RFQ emails. Instead of manually filling quotation forms field by field, paste the email — AI reads it and populates everything in seconds.

  • Extracts agent company, contact, dates, room count, PAX, and rate basis
  • Calculates estimated revenue and suggests optimal rate with rationale
  • Identifies demand period, conditions, and risk factors automatically
  • Generates a ready-to-send reply draft alongside the quotation
  • One-click export of the completed quotation summary
Built-in Marketing
Multi-Channel Campaign Engine

Most CRMs require a separate tool for marketing. SuiteSupports CRM includes a full campaign builder with your guest database already connected — no data export, no third-party cost.

  • Visual HTML email builder with image uploads and preview
  • SMS campaigns for short, high-open-rate promotional messages
  • WhatsApp campaigns with text and optional image attachments
  • Guest database filtering by VIP status, segment, or contactability
  • Campaign archive — duplicate, edit, or delete previous sends
