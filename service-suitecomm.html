How It Works

One Tap to Talk.
AI Does the Rest.

SuiteComm removes the friction of walkie-talkies, WhatsApp chains, and handwritten slips. Staff speak or type in plain language — the platform handles routing, tracking, escalation, and reporting automatically.

1
Speak or Type

Staff tap the mic and speak naturally — "two towels room 1205, urgent" — or type a request. Voice transcript stays on the device; only the text is sent to the server.

2
AI Parses & Routes

DeepSeek AI reads the text, identifies the department (Housekeeping / Engineering / F&B / Front Office), task type, location, and urgency level — in under a second.

3
Instant Push

Socket.IO delivers the task in real time to every device in the target department. The Pending tab badge lights up immediately — no refresh needed.

4
Acknowledge & Complete

The receiving team taps to acknowledge, then marks done with an optional note. Every action is timestamped and logged in the audit trail.

5
SLA & Escalation

If a task isn't acknowledged within 30 minutes, the system automatically emails the department head — so urgent requests never go unattended during busy periods.

Platform Features

Everything a Hotel Ops
Team Actually Needs

Built specifically for hotel operations — not adapted from a generic ticketing system. Every feature is designed around the real pace, workflows, and pressures of a hotel floor.

Voice-First Input

Staff speak naturally — AI parses the intent, fills the form, and flags the right department. No learning curve. The mic button degrades gracefully to text if voice isn't available in the browser.

30-Min SLA Escalation

An automated worker scans for unacknowledged tasks every 60 seconds. If a task passes its SLA window without action, the department head is emailed immediately — automatically, every time.

Real-Time Push

Socket.IO broadcasts every new task and every status change to the target department's devices the moment it happens. Live badge counts update without any manual refresh.

Photo Defect Reports

Housekeeping staff photograph a maintenance issue — broken AC, water leak, furniture damage. AI identifies the defect category, severity, and the correct department to assign it to automatically.

AI Shift Handover

One click generates a structured shift-handover summary for any department — open tasks, completed work, and patterns from the last N hours. Managers walk in briefed, not guessing.

Admin Dashboard

Counts per department × state, tasks past SLA highlighted in red, and a handover generator per department. Department heads and admins see the full picture across the hotel in one view.

Connected Departments

Four Teams.
One Platform.

SuiteComm connects your entire hotel operations team — every inter-department request flows through a single, trackable, AI-routed channel instead of scattered walkie-talkie calls and WhatsApp threads.

Front Office

Raise guest requests, room moves, VIP amenities, and late check-outs. Instantly notifies Housekeeping, Engineering, or F&B with the guest's location and priority.

Housekeeping

Receive and action room requests with live task counts. Use the defect camera to photograph and auto-route maintenance issues to Engineering without leaving the floor.

Food & Beverage

Receive in-room dining requests, setup instructions, and VIP amenity orders from Front Office with full context — room number, guest name, and priority — all pre-filled by AI.

Engineering

Receive photo-tagged defect reports classified by AI — AC, plumbing, electrical, furniture — with severity level assigned automatically so the right technician is dispatched first.

Automation That Protects Your Standards

Nothing Gets
Missed. Ever.

Two built-in automations ensure operational standards are maintained even during peak hours — without requiring any manager to manually chase task status.

SLA Enforcement
Automatic Escalation at 30 Minutes

A background worker scans for unacknowledged tasks every 60 seconds. The moment a task passes its SLA window, the department head receives an email with full task details and a direct link.

  • Configurable SLA time per task (default 30 minutes)
  • Email goes to the department head automatically — no manual trigger
  • Escalation is idempotent — one email per task, no spam
  • Escalation logged in the full task audit trail
  • Socket.IO also emits a task.escalated event to connected devices
AI-Powered
Shift Handover in One Click

At the end of every shift, admins and department heads generate an AI-written handover summary for any department — covering every open task, completed item, and operational pattern from the past N hours.

  • 2–4 sentence narrative summary of the shift
  • Bullet highlights: unfinished items first, then notable patterns
  • Selectable by department and time window
  • Generated by DeepSeek AI from live task data — no manual writing
  • Incoming managers are briefed before they reach the floor
Built to Last

Enterprise Architecture,
Practical Scale

SuiteComm is built on a modern, production-grade stack deployable on any server or cloud platform — with no vendor lock-in and a clean upgrade path to S3 storage and managed databases.

Backend
Node.js + Express

REST API with JWT auth, multipart photo upload, parameterised Postgres queries, Helmet, CORS, rate limiting. Production-ready from day one.

Real-time
Socket.IO

Authenticated WebSocket rooms per department and per user. Tasks push instantly on create, acknowledge, complete, and escalate — across every connected device.

Frontend
React PWA (Vite)

Mobile-first Progressive Web App. Installable on Android and iOS home screen. Works offline for read operations. Voice input via browser Web Speech API.

Database
PostgreSQL

Full audit trail in task_events, fast filtering with indexed task_state, and a polling escalation worker using SELECT FOR UPDATE SKIP LOCKED.

AI
DeepSeek API

Three AI functions: natural language task parsing, photo-based defect classification, and shift handover generation. Strict JSON schema validation with Zod on every response.

Deployment
Docker + Cloud Ready

Docker Compose for local and staging. Caddy for TLS termination. Storage abstraction supports local disk (dev) and S3/MinIO (production) with a single config flag.
