How It Works

Scan, Choose,
Done. The Hotel Plans Around It.

SuiteGreen turns the in-room QR card into a complete guest preference channel. No app downloads, no front-office calls, no door hangers. Five steps from scan to a measurable saving.

1
Guest Scans QR

The guest opens the QR card in their room. SuiteGreen recognises the room, greets them by name, and shows their stay window — all from a single secure token. No login required.

2
Picks Privacy Days

“Do not clean my room” is the lead option. The guest ticks any combination of remaining nights — today, tomorrow, the night before they leave — and submits in one tap.

3
Live to Dashboard

The Housekeeping dashboard updates instantly. Supervisors see the guest name, room, the days picked, and any notes. Late requests are flagged automatically based on the property cut-off.

4
Attendant Plans Ahead

An “Upcoming preferences · next 7 days” panel groups every future privacy day by date so the attendant can brief the team and re-plan linen and water use before the shift starts.

5
Savings Reported

Every accepted request rolls into a sustainability report — estimated linen sets saved, towel sets saved, labour minutes saved — with CSV export ready for the monthly review.

Platform Features

Everything Your Floor
Team Actually Needs

Designed around how hotel housekeeping actually runs — the morning cut-off, the per-floor plan, the supervisor handover, the monthly sustainability report — not a generic survey tool reshaped into a hospitality skin.

Secure In-Room QR

Every room has a rotatable, unguessable token (sg_…). The token never exposes the room number publicly. Disable or rotate per room in one click — no reprinting required for the full floor.

Multi-Day Picker

Guests tick any combination of remaining stay nights in one screen — instead of submitting a fresh request every morning. Less friction for the guest, less repeat work for Front Office.

Property Cut-Off Aware

A configurable daily cut-off (default 09:30) marks late same-day requests with an amber pill. Future days are unaffected. Supervisors decide which late requests can still be actioned.

Sustainability Reporting

Configurable per-option savings (linen sets, towel sets, labour minutes) roll up into daily and monthly reports — defensible numbers for ESG, the brand standard, or owner reviews. CSV export included.

Daily CSV Export

One click downloads the full day’s requests with room, guest name, preference, late flag, status, and notes. Perfect for shift handover, printed floor sheets, or your existing reporting flow.

Role-Based Access

Admin, Housekeeping Supervisor, Attendant, Front Office, Executive — every role sees only what it should. Guest data is minimal and audit-logged on every change.

Built to Last

Modern Stack,
Pilot-Ready

SuiteGreen Housekeeping ships as a pluggable Next.js application — runs standalone for a one-floor pilot today, scales to the full property when you’re ready, integrates with Shiji or SuiteSupports PMS Cloud later.

Frontend
Next.js 15 + React 19

App Router, server components, mobile-first design. Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans typography matching the SuiteSupports brand book. Loads fast, looks luxury.

Database
PostgreSQL · Pluggable

Local JSON store for instant pilots, Supabase Postgres for production. Same schema, single env-var switch — pilot today, go live the next month with no rewrite.

Auth & Security
Role-Based · Audit-Logged

Staff log in with Supabase Auth. Five roles (Admin, Supervisor, Attendant, Front Office, Executive). Every important action writes to an immutable audit log.

QR Tokens
Unguessable · Rotatable

Per-room URL-safe tokens. Never expose the room number publicly. Rotate or disable any QR card in one click — without reprinting the entire floor.

PMS-Ready
Shiji / SS PMS Cloud

Built standalone for the pilot, but the schema is PMS-ready. Stayover detection, room status sync, and guest verification all wire in cleanly when you’re ready.

Deployment
Vercel · Cloud-Ready

Deploys to Vercel out of the box. Custom domain such as green.suitesupports.pro sits cleanly alongside this site. QR cards point straight at the subdomain.
